## Troubleshooting

### Button Not Showing?
- Refresh the page
- Make sure you're on a job application page
- Check that extension is enabled

### Fields Not Filling?
- Ensure your profile is complete
- Some sites use custom forms that need special handling
- Try clicking the button again

### Wrong Information?
- Update your profile in the extension
- Clear and refill the form
